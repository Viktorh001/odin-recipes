Abacha recipe

Abacha is also one of nigeria's favourite recipes,not just only in nigeria,its an african food.as different.

It is pretty simple to prepare,it takes 10-30 minutes to prepare this recipe.

Ingredients

  • cassava
  • palm oil
  • onions
  • fresh pepper
  • salt
  • meat/fish
  • utazi leaf

Steps

  1. boil your meat/fish for 15-20minutes.
  2. put enough palm oil into an empty pot.
  3. put meat/fish.
  4. add other ingredients.
  5. add water,steer and allow to done.
