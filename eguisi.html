Eguisi soup

Eguisi soup is one of nigeria's favourite soup,not just only in nigeria,its an african food.as different tribes have their own method of preparing this soup.

It is pretty simple to prepare,it takes 30-40 minutes to prepare this dish.

Ingredients

  • eguisi
  • palm oil
  • onions
  • fresh pepper
  • water
  • meat/fish
  • bitter leaf

Steps

  1. boil your meat/fish for 15-20minutes.
  2. put enough palm oil into an empty pot.
  3. allow the oil to steam and fry eguisi.
  4. put meat/fish and fry too.
  5. add other ingredients and fry.
  6. add water,steer and allow to done.
