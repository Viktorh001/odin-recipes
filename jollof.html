jollof rice recipe

jollof rice is one of nigeria's favourite recipe,not just only in nigeria,its an african food.as different tribes have their own method of preparing this recipe.

It is pretty simple to prepare,it takes 30-40 minutes to prepare this recipe.

Ingredients

  • rice
  • groundnut oil
  • onions
  • fresh pepper
  • water
  • meat/fish
  • curry leaf
  • salt/tomateos/seasonings

Steps

  1. put enough oil into an empty dry pot.
  2. boil your meat/fish for 15-20minutes.
  3. allow the oil to steam and fry.
  4. put meat/fish and fry too.
  5. add other ingredients and fry.
  6. add water,steer and allow to done.
